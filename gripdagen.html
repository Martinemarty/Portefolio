Carpe Diem

Carpe Diem a mother-daughter lamp for the demented in nursing homes. Which should contribute to coping, security and well-being.


The mother lamp (foot) provides spacious light while the daughter lamp provides direct light.



Project duration: 3 Months
Course: Introduction to design 2.semester
Task: Lamp for the elderly(collaboration with Glamox)
Collaboration: Jenny Duus & Jakob Skarveland
Rapport: Carpe Diem
Programs: Indeisgn, Phototshop & Illustrator

Carpe Diem is designed with strong color contrasts for reduced vision.











The round shape is soft and has no sharp edges. Which gives a caring and comfortable shape
