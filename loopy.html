Loopy

Loopy is a toy rental service for children that offers a sustainable alternative to gifts and makes donating used toys easy.

The service consists of an app, a website and a physical playroom where children can play with our toys and exchange them.



Project duration: 3 Months
Course: Service design 5.semester
Task: make a service, theme: "sustain this"
Collaboration: Carl Mangus Tranberg & Nora Grotberg
Rapport: The book of loopy(Norwegian)
Programs: Adobe XD, Figma, Photoshop, Indesign,
Illustrator & Premierpro
